
ChaiLights is a Flask web app that connects to your Google Photos account and shows you photos taken on specific Hebrew calendar dates.

Want to see what you were doing on this day in past Jewish years? Curious about your photos from Passover or Purim? ChaiLights automatically converts Gregorian photo dates to Hebrew dates and lets you explore your Jewish year in pictures.

---

## 🚀 Features

- 🔗 **Google Login** with OAuth
- 🗓️ **See photos by Hebrew date** – today’s or any you choose
- 💡 **Holiday links** to see photos from:
  - 🎭 Purim
  - 🐸 Passover
  - 🛖 Sukkot
  - 📜 Shavuot
  - 📯 Rosh Hashanah
  - 🤍 Yom Kippur
  - 🇮🇱 Yom Ha'atzmaut
  - 🎖️ Yom HaZikaron
  - 🕍 Yom Yerushalayim
  - 🌳 Tu BiShvat
- 🧠 **Smart options**:
  - ✅ *Include Erev Chag* (day before)
  - ✅ *Outside of Israel* (adds 8th day of holidays)
